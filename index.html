About the Mosque

Our mission is to unite, educate, and uplift.

Central Mosque Ridwanullah is a welcoming place for worshippers from Ikorodu and beyond. We provide daily prayers, Islamic classes, youth outreach, and social support for families.

Daily Prayers

Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib and Isha in a serene and peaceful environment.

Community Support

Support programmes for families, children and new converts in Ikorodu.

Religious Classes

Qur'an lessons, Tafsir sessions, and Islamic studies for all ages.

Our Programmes

Activities for spiritual growth and community wellbeing.

Saturday Tafsir

Weekly Qur'an reflection and guidance for stronger faith.

Youth Circle

Empowering young Muslims with mentorship and civic engagement.

Charity Drives

Food distribution, zakat collection and outreach for the needy.
